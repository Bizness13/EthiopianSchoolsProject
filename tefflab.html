TeffLab

In loving memory of Sasu. A tribute to his life, his love for heritage, and a vision that turned purpose into action.

About Sasu

From a young age, Sasu’s parents instilled in him a deep love for Ethiopian heritage, community, and learning. Family, culture, and curiosity shaped the foundation of his life. Childhood moments—being read to by his parents, exploring food and farming, and asking thoughtful questions—became seeds for a lifelong mission.

Roots: Family, Heritage, and Curiosity

Early Inspiration

Experiences like time spent in fields and farms sparked Sasu’s fascination with agriculture, healthy eating, and the connection between land, people, and nourishment.

Community gatherings reinforced the importance of giving back—an idea that would later define TeffLab’s mission.

From Vision to TeffLab

While studying and later presenting his ideas at NYU, Sasu envisioned a social enterprise rooted in Ethiopian teff—the iron-rich supergrain. TeffLab was born as both an innovation lab and a vehicle for impact.

The TeffBar

TeffBar became TeffLab’s flagship product—crafted, sold at farmers markets and pop-ups, and designed to be both nourishing and purposeful.

Proceeds from TeffBar directly supported the Ethiopian Schools Project, turning everyday purchases into educational opportunity.

Legacy

Sasu’s legacy lives on through TeffLab, TeffBar, and the students whose lives are touched by education and opportunity. This page honors his memory and the love shared by his parents, family, and community.
