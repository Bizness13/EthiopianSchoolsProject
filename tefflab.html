TeffLab

TeffLab is Sasu’s innovation work launched after NYU—exploring new ways to bring teff-powered products to market while supporting education in Ethiopia.

Support Education

Why Teff

Teff is an ancient grain with strong nutrition credentials. TeffLab’s work focuses on modern, convenient formats that help more people discover teff through great-tasting products.

  • Plant-forward, ingredient-led product development
  • Storytelling rooted in Ethiopian food culture
  • Built for modern mobile-first discovery

Give Back Model

TeffLab and TeffBar are purpose-driven: product sales are designed to support the Ethiopian Schools Project and rural education initiatives.
