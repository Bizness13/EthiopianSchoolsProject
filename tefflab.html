TeffLab

In loving memory of Sasu. honoring his life, his love for Ethiopian heritage, and a vision that turned purpose into action—supporting education through TeffLab and the TeffBar.

Early Cultivation and Inspiration

Sasu’s parents instilled in him a deep love for Ethiopian heritage, community, and learning. That foundation shaped his curiosity—about food, farming, and the ways culture can inspire innovation and impact.

Cultivation Takes Root

With the grounding of family and heritage, Sasu’s curiosity kept expanding—toward farming, healthy eating, and the joy of cooking. Moments like these became part of the story that later grew into TeffLab.

Heritage, health, and experimentation

From an early age he was drawn to the relationship between food and wellbeing—how ingredients, culture, and community come together. That curiosity would later shape TeffLab’s mission and the creation of TeffBar.

TeffBar

TeffBar was TeffLab’s flagship product—crafted, shared at farmers markets and pop-ups, and designed to be both nourishing and purposeful. Proceeds helped support Ethiopian Schools Project.

Farmers Market & Pop-Ups

TeffBar was sold at farmers markets and community events—creating space for conversation, culture, and support for education.

Watch Farmers Market Video

Legacy

Sasu’s work through TeffLab and TeffBar supported the Ethiopian Schools Project’s mission—strengthening literacy, improving learning spaces, and helping remove barriers so students can thrive. Sasu’s legacy lives on through TeffLab, TeffBar, and the students whose lives are touched by education and opportunity. TeffLab was more than a project—it was an expression of identity and service.
