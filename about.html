About Ethiopian Schools Project

We are a Seattle-based nonprofit partnering with communities in Ethiopia to strengthen education through practical, locally-led projects.

Our mission

To expand access to quality education by supporting literacy, improving learning spaces, and removing barriers that keep students from school.

How we work

  • Community partnership: local stakeholders shape priorities and scope
  • Practical delivery: durable improvements that can be maintained locally
  • Transparency: clear reporting and measurable outcomes

Founder story & history

Founded in 2002, Ethiopian Schools Project began with a simple idea: practical support—books, classrooms, and materials—can meaningfully improve a student’s chance to learn and succeed. Over time, the organization has grown through community partnerships and supporters who believe education is the most durable path to opportunity.
