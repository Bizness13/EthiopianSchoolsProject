TeffBar & TeffLabs

TeffBar is a purpose-driven Ethiopian-American snack foods project powered by teff. Its mission ties food entrepreneurship to impact—supporting rural education in Ethiopia and helping fund essential school supplies through the Ethiopian Schools Project.

TeffLabs expands this work—bringing the story, culture, and future of teff to new audiences. These videos introduce the journey and why it matters.
