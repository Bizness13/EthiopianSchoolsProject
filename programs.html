How project sites are selected

We prioritize communities with limited learning materials and inadequate classrooms, and we coordinate with local stakeholders to align on needs and delivery.

  • Local partnership: community and school leadership co-designs scope
  • Practical logistics: materials and construction that can be maintained locally
  • Measurable outcomes: classroom capacity, access to books, and attendance support
